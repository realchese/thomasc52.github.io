About

I’m Thomas Chan, a freshman at UC Berkeley studying the social sciences and humanities.

I write about the things that bother me: the gap between how we talk about people and how people actually live, the systems we build and then forget we built, the moments where theory runs out and reality takes over.

Most of my writing lives on Substack. This site collects the essays I’m most proud of.

If you want to get in touch, find me there.
